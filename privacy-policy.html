Effective Date: June 2025

At GoldRateCalculator.in, we respect your privacy and are committed to maintaining a very simple and transparent user experience. This privacy policy outlines what data we collect — which is practically none — and how we use it.

1. No Personal Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or track any personal information from our users. No login is required, and no names, emails, or contact numbers are requested at any point.

2. Input Data is Never Stored

The data you enter into the calculator — such as weight, making charges, or city — stays only in your browser. It is never sent to a server or stored anywhere.

We use Google Analytics to understand general usage patterns — such as how many users visited the site, which pages were viewed, and how long users stayed. This data is aggregated and anonymized. It does not include any personal identifiers.

5. Cookies

We do not use any cookies for personalization or advertising. However, third-party services like Google Analytics may use cookies to provide their functionality.
